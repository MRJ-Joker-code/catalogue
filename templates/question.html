Questions & Answers

What is CATALOGUE?
CATALOGUE is a platform where users and businesses can promote their products, services, and content through engaging and stylish ads.
Is it free to use?
Yes, browsing and viewing content is free. Some advanced promotional features may require payment or account upgrades.
How do I post an ad?
Simply log in, go to your dashboard, and click on "upload". You’ll be guided through uploading media, writing a description, and setting visibility.
Who can see my ads?
Ads are visible to all users, unless you choose to make them private or target specific groups (coming soon).
How do I contact support?
You can visit our Contact page or email us directly at support@catalogue.com.
